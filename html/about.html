About

G’day, my name is Gerry, the founder of Green.En.Kids.

I care deeply about our natural environment. I am passionate about ensuring our ecosystems are preserved and sustained so that future generations can enjoy the many benefits it has to offer.

It is essential that we equip our young people with the knowledge and skills to care for the natural environment. But more importantly to inspire and empower them to make a difference today and into the future.

I believe that music is a universal gift, a language that speaks to everyone, a powerful way to convey meaningful messages that strikes a chord on so many levels.

Through Green.En.Kids, I hope to plant the seeds of curiosity, care, and action in young minds—so they grow into passionate protectors of our planet. This is the legacy I want to leave behind: a generation of kids who understand the value of nature and are inspired to make a lasting difference.
